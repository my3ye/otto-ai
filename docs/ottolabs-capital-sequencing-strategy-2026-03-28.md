## The Sovereignty Constraint

All three phases are designed around a non-negotiable structural rule inherited from the MY3YE mission:

> **Capital must serve the system, not own it.**

This means:
- No investor receives governance majority over any project
- Revenue distributions are liquid; governance rights are contribution-earned (non-buyable)
- Every capital event is publicly verifiable on-chain (multisig + OPRLP milestone attestations)
- CET (Contribution Equity Tokens) are soulbound — labor equity cannot be diluted by capital raises
- Agent tax (30%) flows back to contributors — automation gains are redistributed, not extracted

These constraints are structural, not aspirational. They are enforced at the smart contract layer and are disclosed to every capital partner before term sheet.

## On-Chain Transparency Architecture (Cross-Phase)

This is the structural trust mechanism that de-risks investor relationships across all three phases. It is not a dashboard bolt-on — it is built into the contract architecture from Phase 1.

### Mechanism 1: On-Chain Treasury with Milestone Gates

Every capital raise uses a multi-tranche disbursement structure tied to on-chain milestone attestations:

```
Investor → TransactionPool → Milestone Attestation → Tranche Release
```

- Investor commits capital to a multisig treasury contract (3-of-5 signatories)
- Each tranche is gated behind a milestone attestation from the OPRLP milestone registry
- Milestone attestations require multi-party verification (project team + independent validator + community oracle)
- No milestone attestation = no tranche release — the investor can verify this directly on-chain

**Investor benefit:** Capital cannot be misappropriated before milestone delivery. No need to trust the team — trust the contract.

### Mechanism 2: DPC Contributor Registry (Labor Accountability)

Every worker who contributes to a Phase build earns CET (Contribution Equity Token) and DPC score on-chain via the Labor Attestation contracts. This provides:

- Public proof that human labor is the foundation of physical assets (not just investor capital)
- Verifiable record of who built what and when — insurance against future ownership disputes
- Soulbound CET tokens prove equity distribution to workers without requiring complex legal structures
- 30% agent tax enforced on-chain — automation gains are verifiably redistributed, not extracted

**Investor benefit:** Demonstrates non-extractive governance model is structurally enforced, not just a whitepaper claim.

### Mechanism 3: Revenue Transparency (Real-Time)

- WebAssist MRR tracked via Stripe + publicly logged on-chain monthly (privacy-safe aggregate)
- $KOIN community treasury: all movements require on-chain governance vote, visible to any holder
- Ottolabs RaaS revenue: reported quarterly, tied to on-chain milestone verification
- Tusita revenue: green bond servicing + carbon credit distributions all on-chain

**Investor benefit:** No quarterly report to believe or disbelieve. Data is on-chain and queryable.

### Mechanism 4: Governance Non-Concentration Protection

Built into every investment agreement from Phase 2 onwards:

- No investor controls > 5% of any project's governance weight without DPC contribution
- Governance weight is contribution-earned (DPC), not capital-proportional
- SAFE conversions give economic participation, not governance control
- Community governance treasury ($KOIN) cannot be sold to a single entity

This is standard in every term sheet. Investors who reject it are screened out — that is the point.
