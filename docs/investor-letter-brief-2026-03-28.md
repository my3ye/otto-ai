## VI. ANTI-EXTRACTION ARCHITECTURE (Core Differentiator)

These are structural — not aspirational. Use these as proof points:

1. **On-Chain Treasury with Milestone Gates** — Investor capital enters treasury. Each tranche releases only on attested milestone completion. Multi-party verification. No milestone, no release. Verify on-chain.

2. **CET — Contribution Equity Tokens** — Physical workers earn soulbound tokens. Cannot be sold or diluted by investor SAFEs. Workers earn quarterly stablecoin income from the assets they built. Protocol-enforced, not promised.

3. **30% Agent Tax** — When AI agents complete tasks that would otherwise require human labor, 30% of efficiency gain flows back to contributor pool. Automation gains do not go to capital.

4. **Governance Non-Concentration Clause** — No investor controls >5% governance without DPC contribution. SAFEs convert to economic participation, not governance control.

5. **Separate entity raises** — No bundled sale of ecosystem control. Investors buy into specific projects, not MY3YE as a whole.
