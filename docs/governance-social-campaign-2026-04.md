## Strategic Frame

This campaign runs after the Web3 inbound calendar (Mar 27–Apr 3) and the existing ecosystem reveals calendar. It serves a different function: **depth over breadth**.

The inbound calendar built builder credibility. The governance campaign establishes MY3YE as a source of original thinking on a problem that matters to every serious Web3 participant: who actually controls these systems?

The campaign does three things:
1. **Defines the enemy** — not villains, but structural failure modes (centralization and governance theater)
2. **Presents the mechanism** — DPC, rotation, decay, on-chain constraints
3. **Opens the door** — invites builders to participate in building the protocol, not just consuming it

---

## Narrative Arc

```
Problem → Question → Mechanism → Proof → Invitation
```

**Week 1 — The Problem & The Question (Apr 4–9)**
Open with the framing that makes people want to read further. Every leader in the space has tried governance; most have been disappointed. This week names why.

**Week 2 — The Mechanism & The Proof (Apr 11–17)**
Shift to concrete specifics. DPC, council tiers, Solidity contracts. The audience that survived Week 1 is ready for this. The audience that skipped Week 1 will be attracted by the specificity.
