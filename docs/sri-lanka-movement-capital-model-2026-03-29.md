### The DPC Score — How Contribution Becomes Governance Weight

The Decentralized Participation Contribution (DPC) score is the mechanism that separates this from a standard fundraise. You can buy $KOIN (capital exposure) but you cannot buy DPC score (governance amplification). DPC is earned.

**DPC Formula (contribution-weighted):**

```
DPC_score = Σ(contribution_value × quality_multiplier × time_multiplier × scarcity_multiplier)

contribution_value = verified economic + social + technical contribution
quality_multiplier = 1.0x (base) → 3.0x (exceptional, peer-reviewed)
time_multiplier = 2.0x (Genesis), 1.5x (Founders), 1.0x (Community)
scarcity_multiplier = inverse of contributor count in that role at time of contribution
```

**What DPC unlocks:**

| DPC Tier | Score | Rights |
|---|---|---|
| Observer | < 100 | Read governance, participate in public forums |
| Contributor | 100-1,000 | Vote with $KOIN (1x weight), access contributor channels |
| Builder | 1,000-10,000 | Vote at 2x weight, propose governance changes, revenue share eligible |
| Architect | 10,000-100,000 | Vote at 3x weight, Joint Council eligible, Tusita founding governance rights |
| Founder | 100,000+ | Vote at 5x weight, permanent Joint Council seat, BTC reserve governance input |

**The DPC score is a permanent record.** It cannot be transferred, sold, or inflated. It is a verifiable on-chain history of what you contributed to the civilization before it existed.
