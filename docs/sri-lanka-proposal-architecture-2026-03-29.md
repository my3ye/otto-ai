### 5C. Constitutional Boundaries (Immutable)

These cannot be changed by either side without dissolving the agreement:

1. **Capital never governs.** Investment buys no governance weight in MY3YE. DPC score comes from verified contribution only. This protects the movement from capture — including by Sri Lanka's political interests.

2. **Sri Lanka retains full sovereignty.** MY3YE operates WITHIN Sri Lankan law. No extraterritorial claims. No parallel legal system. Port City SEZ provides the legal framework; MY3YE doesn't replace it.

3. **On-chain transparency for all joint funds.** Every dollar of green bond money, FDI, and SDEZ revenue is auditable on public ledger. This is the IMF compatibility mechanism.

4. **Right of exit for both sides.** Sri Lanka can revoke the SDEZ framework with 12-month notice. MY3YE can withdraw operations with 6-month notice. Assets and IP return to their respective owners. No hostage dynamics.

5. **Contributor protections are paramount.** The 50,000 people who build this system cannot lose their earned governance weight, revenue share, or identity credentials if the agreement dissolves. Their DPC scores and ONEON identities are sovereign to them.

6. **Founder sunset.** Per OPRLP architecture: founding team governance weight decays over 4 years until it matches earned contribution. Mev and MY3YE founders have NO permanent governance premium.

### 5D. Dispute Resolution

```
Operational disputes → MY3YE internal (SOS arbitration)
SDEZ policy disputes → Joint Governance Council (5/9 quorum)
Legal disputes → Sri Lanka commercial courts (Port City jurisdiction)
Constitutional violations → Independent tribunal (3 members: 1 SL, 1 MY3YE, 1 multilateral)
```
