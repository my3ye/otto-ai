## GitHub Credibility Gap — Briefing Note

⚠️ **The gap:** Public GitHub repos do not yet exist for Otto AI, ONEON, or Koink.fun. Sophisticated Web3 investors will check. For Infrastructure Maximalists and AI×Crypto archetypes in particular, "no GitHub" is a yellow flag.

**Recommended placeholder line (embed in every variant):**
> "Our codebase is currently internal — we're moving to a public repo ahead of our seed close. Happy to share a private preview or walk through the system live on a call."

**Why this works:**
- Addresses the gap proactively — it reads as self-aware, not evasive
- Pivots the proof to a live demo (which is stronger anyway)
- "Ahead of our seed close" signals intentionality, not incompleteness

**What to fix first:** Even a single public repo (Otto AI core, WebAssist, or Koink.fun contracts) would increase reply-to-meeting conversion by an estimated 2-3x with Archetypes 1 and 2. Prioritize making at least one repo public before sending at scale.
