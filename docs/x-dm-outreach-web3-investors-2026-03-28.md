## Usage Rules

- **Personalize the opener** — swap bracketed fields before sending. Generic first messages are ignored.
- **Send to the right variant** — Variant 1 for AI×Crypto funds, Variant 2 for serial angels, Variant 3 for ownership economy VCs.
- **Never attach a deck to the first message** — link to demo or live product only.
- **Reply to one of their recent posts before DMing** — warm the path for 2-3 days.
- **One DM per account** — do not multi-channel (DM + email + LinkedIn) simultaneously; it reads as desperation.

**Follow-up rules:**

- Always add ONE new concrete data point — shows shipping cadence, which is the signal these investors want.
- Keep the tone calm — no urgency theater ("we're closing soon," "limited spots").
- If they liked or replied to any of your posts in the 5 days, reference that instead of sending this cold follow-up.
