### Structure: Tusita Community Land Pass

**Instrument:** $TUSITA Community Land Pass — NFT-gated fractional land governance rights
**What holders get:**
- Fractional governance rights over Phase 0 land parcel
- Access rights (stay credits per tier, non-transferable to outside community)
- Contribution Score that compounds with on-site labor and other contributions
- Priority access to residency tier upgrades (Islander → Steward → Founder)

**Tier Structure:**

| Tier | Entry | What You Get | On-chain |
|------|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Islander Pass** | $50 (or $TUSITA equivalent) | Governance rights for 0.01% land stake. 1 night/year stay access. | ERC-721 on Base or Solana |
| **Steward Pass** | $500 | 0.1% land stake. 5 nights/year. Community vote rights on site plans. | ERC-721 |
| **Founder Pass** | $5,000 | 1% land stake. 30 nights/year. Multi-sig governance seat for Phase 0. | ERC-721 |
| **Sovereign Pass** | $25,000 | 5% land stake. Full residency rights. Founding Sovereign status. | ERC-721 |

**Target Round:**
- Islander: 500 passes × $50 = $25K
- Steward: 100 passes × $500 = $50K
- Founder: 20 passes × $5,000 = $100K
- Sovereign: 4 passes × $25,000 = $100K
- **Total target: $275K community round**

**Blockchain Abstraction (Critical):**
Community members should not need to understand blockchain to participate. The entry UX must be:
1. Visit tusita.xyz/join
2. Choose your tier
3. Pay with credit card, PayPal, or stablecoin (all accepted)
4. A wallet is automatically created (non-custodial, using account abstraction)
5. Your Land Pass NFT is minted to your wallet automatically
6. You receive a confirmation email with wallet recovery details
7. All governance participation happens via the Tusita web app — no MetaMask required

**Technology stack for abstraction:**
- Account abstraction: Privy.io or Biconomy (email/social login → auto wallet)
- Payment gateway: Stripe + USDC stablecoin bridge (handled by Transak or MoonPay on the backend)
- NFT minting: ERC-721 on Base (low gas, fast confirmation)
- Governance: snapshot.org integration (gasless voting via signature)
