# #BuildWithOtto Campaign Brief
**Model:** Mirrors EasyA's #180DaysOfPolkadot — a named, persistent campaign tied to the MY3YE University program on EasyA.

---

## Core Concept

**Tagline:** "What you build doesn't have to die on Monday."

**Hook:** Every hackathon has a graveyard — projects that worked, then expired. APIs revoked. Teams dispersed. Infrastructure abandoned. #BuildWithOtto is the campaign for builders who want their work to compound. Build on infrastructure that remembers. Contribution tracked. Work that outlives the weekend.

---

## Campaign Structure

### Phase 1 — Consensus Miami 2026 (Launch)
- One dedicated track at EasyA × Consensus Miami 2026
- Challenge: "Build an AI agent that serves someone who was handed nothing"
- Leaderboard: real-time scoring based on project submissions
- Prizes: $14,000 cash pool + ecosystem grants for top 3
- Social: participants post progress with #BuildWithOtto — weekly featured builds on MY3YE channels

### Phase 2 — MY3YE University on EasyA (6-12 months)
Three modules available in-app:
- **Module 1: Otto AI** — Zero to autonomous agent. Build an agent with persistent memory in one session.
- **Module 2: ONEON Identity** — Build sovereign identity applications. Portable reputation. Verifiable contribution.
- **Module 3: SOS Systems — Build for Impact** — DAO tooling, resource coordination, community governance. Build for people who cannot afford to wait.

Milestone rewards: cash prizes at completion checkpoints (mirrors #180DaysOfPolkadot structure).

### Phase 3 — University Campus Events
Co-hosted hackathon at Harvard, Oxford, or Cambridge (one of EasyA's partner universities).
- 100–300 developers
- MY3YE as primary sponsor
- Focus: AI agents for social good
- Judges: MY3YE team + invited AI researchers

---

## Content Pillars for #BuildWithOtto

1. **"The Graveyard" series** — showcase of hackathon projects that almost shipped but didn't. What was missing: infrastructure that remembers. (Tone: respectful, factual, motivating)

2. **"Build in Public" series** — weekly feature of #BuildWithOtto developers. What they built. How they used Otto AI. What it does today.

3. **"For the Ones Who Were Handed Nothing"** — the SOS Systems narrative. EasyA's social impact audience. Real problems. Real infrastructure.

4. **"The Architecture"** — technical deep dives for developers. How Otto AI's memory, task queue, and kernel actually work. Written for builders, not marketers.
