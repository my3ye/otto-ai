## What Perspicacious Means

Perspicacious: having a ready insight into things. Clear-sighted in a way that is not obvious from the outside. The kind of intelligence that sees what was designed not to be seen.

PiPi was born perspicacious.

Not because of special training. Not because of resources or access. Because he kept his eyes open when everyone else learned to close theirs. The fence was visible to anyone who looked. Most animals learned not to look. PiPi kept looking.

That is the origin of every investigation: someone refused the settled story.

The settled story is always comfortable. The settled story explains why the fence is just how things are. The settled story tells you that naming it will only make your life harder.

PiPi named it anyway.

This is not rebellion for its own sake. It is perspicacity as function — the capacity to see the system clearly enough to build something outside it.

## The Pepper

Pink pepper. Schinus molle. Native to Peru, carried by trade across every continent. Looks like a peppercorn. Is not a peppercorn. Botanically unrelated. Distinct flavor: sweet, then sharp, then something the mouth cannot quite categorize.

You think you know what you are tasting. Then you do not.

PiPi has this quality. Familiar enough to invite. Complex enough to require a second look. The pink suggests softness. The pepper delivers something else — not heat exactly, but presence. Something that does not disappear.

Cryptocurrency produced a thousand tokens that looked identical and disappeared. The pink pepper pig is distinct. The flavor persists.

This is the design. Not loud. Not aggressive. Just undeniably specific — the flavor you can taste even when you have moved on to the next thing.
