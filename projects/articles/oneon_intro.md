## Five Layers, One Architecture

ONEON is structured as five concentric layers. You enter from the outside. You go deeper by choice. Each layer requires more commitment and grants more access.

**Layer 1: Public Presence.** The face you choose to show the world. Your handle, your published work, your public contributions to the ecosystem. Discoverable, linkable, yours to manage.

**Layer 2: Community.** The networks and communities you participate in — topic-based, project-based, identity-based. The conversations that require more than public broadcast. The communities where depth happens.

**Layer 3: Governance.** Where the ecosystem's decisions are made. Contribution-weighted governance for every MY3YE project that uses ONEON as its civic infrastructure. Not a vote-farming interface — a genuine deliberation layer with proposals, review, and on-chain execution.

**Layer 4: Encrypted Private.** End-to-end encrypted communications. The layer where privacy is not a premium feature but the default architecture. Keys belong to you. Nobody in the middle.

**Layer 5: Sovereign Self.** Your core identity. The private record that doesn't propagate anywhere, shown to nobody, held by you. The innermost layer — the self that the network exists to protect.

This architecture solves a problem that no existing platform has chosen to solve: how to serve radically different privacy needs within a single coherent system. Some of what you do is public. Some is communal. Some is private. Some belongs only to you. ONEON gives each dimension its own layer rather than flattening everything into a single data profile that serves the platform's analytics.

## The ZEN Network

Three principles underpin the ONEON operating philosophy.

**Zero Extraction.** The network does not monetize your data, your attention, or your identity. There is no advertising layer, no behavioral targeting, no incentive structure that benefits from making you stay longer against your interest. The network serves the participants, not the other way around.

**Every Layer Protected.** Privacy is not a feature you toggle on. It is the default at every layer appropriate to it. The architecture enforces the protection structurally — you cannot accidentally make something public that belongs in your encrypted layer.

**Network for the Long Game.** ONEON is not optimized for growth metrics, engagement numbers, or retention rates. It is optimized for the civilizational timescale — the kind of network that exists in fifty years because it was built on premises that don't collapse when the incentives shift.

The ZEN Network. Three words that describe what every network should have been.

## Memory Capsules — Your Intelligence, Owned by You

Every interaction you have on any network leaves traces. The platforms harvest those traces. They build a model of you, monetize it, and give you nothing back.

ONEON inverts this completely.

Every participant on ONEON has a **Memory Capsule** — a personal, layered intelligence layer that accumulates with every interaction. Private by default. Yours entirely. The capsule holds your preferences, your history of contributions, your learned context, your knowledge graph. It is the record of your mind engaging with this ecosystem.

What makes Memory Capsules different from a data profile on a centralized platform:

**It compounds for you, not against you.** The richer your Memory Capsule, the better the AI outputs you receive. The capsule is not an ad-targeting profile. It is a context engine — the accumulated knowledge the network uses to give you better answers, more relevant connections, more aligned governance suggestions.

**You decide what earns.** Memory Capsules are private by default. But if you choose to share specific layers — your skills, your interest graph, your contribution patterns — you earn for that sharing. The higher the quality and utility of your shared context, the more you earn. Quality determines compensation, not extraction.

**The chain is the neural network.** Each Memory Capsule is an encrypted on-chain record. Not hosted on a server someone else controls. Not deletable by a platform decision. The ONEON chain is the continually-evolving neural network of the ecosystem — every Memory Capsule a node in it. The intelligence of the collective grows as every participant's capsule grows.

**Layered like the network.** Your Memory Capsule mirrors ONEON's architecture: public layers you choose to share, community layers visible to your networks, governance layers that inform your contribution weight, private layers known only to you. The depth you invest in your capsule is the depth you get back.

This is what data should have always been — your asset, compounding in your favor, contributing to something larger only at your explicit choice.
