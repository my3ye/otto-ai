The projects most relevant to Polkadot:

Architecture in progress: [github.com/ottomev/pallet-dpc](https://github.com/ottomev/pallet-dpc)

**Koink.fun** — A chain-agnostic meme tokenomics platform. The $KOINK Standard encodes five anti-whale mechanics at the contract level. Wherever a chain can support the contracts, Koink deploys. Polkadot Hub dApp deployment is in scope.

**PiPi** — Our cultural mascot and the emotional entry point to the ecosystem. A pink pepper pig who has been underestimated, redirected, and kept building. PiPi is PI: π (infinite), PI (investigator), PIP (seed), PI (public interest), PIPI (the echo). ## Why Polkadot

We came to Polkadot because of what it actually is — not what it says it is.

Polkadot is the only major ecosystem where shared security does not require homogeneity. Parachains maintain sovereignty while inheriting relay chain security. That is the architecture. That is also our governance philosophy. ONEON on People Chain is not a coincidence — it is alignment.

Polkadot's OpenGov is the most sophisticated on-chain governance system in production. DPC governance (505 Systems) does not compete with it. It extends the second dimension: contribution weight alongside token weight. Builders earn influence proportional to their demonstrated effort. That is what Polkadot's governance needs to reward the people who actually build it.

Flatline developer growth is a documented community concern. According to the Electric Capital Developer Report, Polkadot has maintained roughly 450–500 monthly active developers since the 2021 peak. MY3YE is a Web3-native community with a self-education system designed to bring skilled contributors from zero to production. We do not just build on Polkadot — we build the pipeline of people who build on Polkadot.
