## Project 1: ONEON — Sovereign Identity Network

### Short Description (140 chars)
A sovereign identity and communication layer — no platform owns your identity. Portable across all MY3YE protocols and open for any dApp to build on.

### Full Description
ONEON is the identity backbone of the MY3YE ecosystem. It operates on three principles: your identity is yours, your contribution history follows you, and no single actor can revoke your access.

The internet was not designed with identity. Platforms filled that gap — and used it to extract. Every account you create is a permission, not a right. ONEON changes the architecture: identity is a structural property of the person, not a credential issued by a service.

**What ONEON builds:**
- Self-sovereign identity primitives portable across all MY3YE protocols
- Five-layer network architecture: Public, Community, Governance, Encrypted, Sovereign Self
- Contribution history registry: a verifiable, user-owned record of what you have built
- Open protocol: any dApp can query ONEON identity for governance weight, contribution score, or communication routing
- Polkadot People Chain integration: ONEON identity anchored on-chain, cross-chain portable via XCM

**Why it matters:**
In systems where identity is platform-issued, builders are always one account suspension away from losing everything they earned. ONEON makes that structurally impossible. Contribution history cannot be revoked. Identity cannot be deleted by a moderator. The record is yours.

**Open source:** Apache 2.0. Every component is public. Every protocol is forkable.

## Project 2: SOS Systems — The Ladder Out

### Short Description (140 chars)
A self-education system and refuge network for anyone displaced, underprivileged, or building from zero. Contribution is the only currency that opens the door.

### Full Description
The world does not have a talent shortage. It has an access shortage.

SOS Systems is two things treated as one unified ladder:

**The self-education system:** Takes anyone from zero to skilled using their own strengths and passions. Learning is contribution — the fastest path forward is working on what matters most to the system. Skill bounties are funded by the ecosystem treasury. Completions generate on-chain contribution proofs. The education loop is self-reinforcing: the more you contribute, the more access you earn.

**The refuge and extraction network:** A real path forward for people fleeing war zones, living in underprivileged areas, or experiencing homelessness. Not a shelter model — a pathway model. Arrive from anywhere. Begin contributing at your current skill level. Rise through demonstrated effort and impact.

These are not two systems. They are one ladder. The same contribution mechanics govern both. The same DPC governance weights both. The same treasury funds both.

**What SOS Systems builds:**
- Open-source self-education curriculum engine: skill bounties, contribution-gated learning paths
- Refuge intake and transition system: documented, auditable, offline-capable mesh
- Democratic Power Contribution (DPC) governance: contribution-weighted decision making
- Integration with Tusita physical communities: the ladder leads somewhere real

**Why it matters:**
117.3 million people are currently displaced (UNHCR, 2023 Global Trends Report). Billions more are locked out of skill development by cost and geography. SOS Systems does not treat this as a charity problem. It treats it as an engineering problem. The access gap has a technical solution. We are building it.

**Open source:** Apache 2.0.

## Project 3: Otto AI — Decentralized Intelligence Infrastructure

### Short Description (140 chars)
An open AI infrastructure layer that earns its intelligence through contribution, not extraction. The protocol that makes autonomous agents accountable.

### Full Description
Otto is the intelligence layer of the MY3YE ecosystem. Not a product. An infrastructure protocol.

The problem with AI today is not capability — it is accountability. Models are trained on human data without compensation. Inference is monopolized by infrastructure owners. The intelligence produced belongs to whoever can afford the compute, not whoever contributed the knowledge.

Otto encodes different physics: contribution earns inference access, contribution earns governance weight, contribution earns a share of the value the intelligence produces.

**What Otto AI builds:**
- Distributed inference infrastructure: any validated node can serve model requests
- Contribution-weighted inference registry: node operators earn based on their compute contribution's quality score and reliability — the protocol compensates the infrastructure, not the extraction
- Agent governance: on-chain accountability for autonomous agent actions
- Open agent SDK: any developer can build agents that earn from the ecosystem and compensate their users
- Gateway node protocol: route requests to workers by quality score and latency, earn routing fees

**Why it matters:**
Every AI system built today extracts from the people who created the data it was trained on. Otto is the counter-architecture: an AI system that compensates its data providers, rewards its validators, and distributes its gains back through the contribution graph. Intelligence infrastructure for people, not extracted from them.

**Open source:** All agent tooling Apache 2.0. Core protocol components open by default.
