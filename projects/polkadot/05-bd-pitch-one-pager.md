## The Ask

We are MY3YE. We build protocols where contribution is the only currency that compounds.

We are entering the Polkadot ecosystem on four tracks simultaneously. We are not asking for investment. We are asking for partnership — co-building the infrastructure Polkadot needs and does not yet have.

---

## What Polkadot Needs. What We Build.

**Gap 1: Contribution-weighted governance**
OpenGov is token-weighted. Builders with years of contribution hold less influence than recent capital entrants. 505 Systems DPC pallet extends OpenGov with a second dimension: earned influence alongside purchased influence. Any parachain can adopt it. We are filing a W3F Level 1 grant today.

**Gap 2: Portable self-sovereign identity**
People Chain is Polkadot's dedicated identity infrastructure. It needs a portable contribution history layer — a record that follows the person across parachains. ONEON's identity pallet builds exactly this. W3F Level 1 grant filing simultaneously with DPC.

**Gap 3: No cultural mascot**
Polkadot has documented this gap internally. The developer community has raised it publicly. Infrastructure without culture does not hold builders. PiPi is our cultural mascot — a pink pepper pig who has been underestimated and kept building. The parallel is not accidental. PiPi is multi-chain by design. We are pitching PiPi as Polkadot's cultural anchor.

**Gap 4: Flat developer pipeline**
450-500 monthly active developers, flat since 2021 (Electric Capital Developer Report). MY3YE is building a self-education system (SOS Systems) that takes people from zero to skilled contributor, with contribution proofs anchoring on People Chain. We do not just build on Polkadot — we grow the pool of people who do.
