## 6. Contingency: If Volume Stalls in First 2 Hours

**Stall definition:** Less than 5% bonding curve reached in 2 hours with trade velocity dropping.

**Triage protocol:**

### If stall is at <5% (first hour dead zone):
**Root cause assessment:** Was the CA shared but buys didn't happen? Was wallet diversity violated? Did bots flood first 50 trades?

**Immediate actions (first 30 min of stall):**
1. Post from @PinkPepePig — high-energy lore post. DO NOT acknowledge stall.
2. Send direct DMs to all pre-committed community members: "reef moment — in now"
3. Call in 2-3 KOLs immediately (wave 2 moved up) — "it's time"
4. Run the meme contest NOW — inject engagement energy

**If still stalling at 90 minutes:**
- Accept: this launch window is not working
- DO NOT abandon the token — stay consistent with posting
- Shift strategy: "slow and steady graduation" frame — "PiPi doesn't rush, he's already immortal"
- Extend timeline from 72 hours to 7 days — reposition as a slow-burn play
- Post memes every 6 hours — low-effort maintenance mode

### If stall is at 10-30% (mid-curve stall):
This is actually a more dangerous position — some community is in and underwater if it dumps.

**Immediate actions:**
1. DO NOT ask people to buy to save others — that's a rug setup psychologically
2. Post lore content only — maintain the narrative
3. Identify which KOLs haven't posted yet — deploy them now
4. CT outreach: share chart + lore to 5-10 accounts that post about Pump.fun plays
5. Consider a strategic buy: Mev adds another wallet buy to create a price movement signal

**If at >50% stall:**
This is a strong position — pivot messaging to "we are going to graduate this" and treat it as a community win goal. The FOMO from being this close to graduation is real. Deploy the 90% protocol immediately (treat 50% as 90%).

### The Nuclear Option (only if truly dead after 4 hours):
- Accept the current launch as a "dry run" — but DO NOT abandon the contract
- Maintain posting for 7 days minimum — some tokens graduate slowly
- Simultaneously begin a "second wave" community recruitment (CT posts about the project)
- Never delete the Telegram or go silent — that signals rug

### What NOT to do under any circumstances:
- Do not post "pls buy" or desperation content — kills community trust instantly
- Do not promise price targets
- Do not blame the community
- Do not go silent — silence = rug signal
- Do not fake volume via circular trading — detectable and ruins graduation
