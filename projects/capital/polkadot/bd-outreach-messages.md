## Key Positioning Points

1. **Not trying to replace Polkadot infrastructure — extending it.** ONEON extends People Chain. 505 Systems extends OpenGov. Koink.fun deploys on Hub. We build with the grain.

2. **The cultural gap is real.** Polkadot has flat developer growth and limited cultural identity. MY3YE fills both — technical infrastructure and cultural presence simultaneously.

3. **Two composable Level 1 grants = $20K, two self-contained deliverables.** ONEON identity + 505 Systems DPC are independent but composable. Together they form a more complete governance stack.

4. **PiPi is the unlock.** Cultural mascots drive community growth. A community that grows feeds the technical ecosystem. Polkadot + PiPi is a narrative play, not just a technical one.

5. **We are builders, not extractors.** Zero raised. No VC backing. Open source everything. Two production products already shipped. The track record is real.
