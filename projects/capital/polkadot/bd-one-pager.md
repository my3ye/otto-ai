# MY3YE x Polkadot
## One-Pager for Ecosystem Partners and W3F

---

**MY3YE** is an open-source ecosystem building decentralized infrastructure across identity, governance, intelligence, and community systems.

We are entering the Polkadot ecosystem with three contributions — and two production products already shipped.

---

## What We Build

### ONEON — Sovereign Identity on People Chain
**What it is:** Portable, user-owned digital identity that cannot be revoked. Built for Polkadot's People Chain. Readable across 50+ parachains via XCM.
**What it adds:** Contribution-weighted reputation — identity that grows richer as the holder builds. The decentralized credential that Web3 needs but does not have.
**Grant path:** W3F Level 1 ($10K) — Core identity pallet + XCM integration
**Web:** oneon.ink

### 505 Systems — Contribution Governance for OpenGov
**What it is:** A Decentralized Proof of Contribution (DPC) module that adds reputation-weighted voting to Polkadot's OpenGov framework.
**What it adds:** Governance power scaled by on-chain contribution history alongside token holdings. Token weight and contribution weight together. A composable module any Substrate chain can integrate.
**Grant path:** W3F Level 1 ($10K) — DPC scoring pallet + OpenGov integration

### Koink.fun — Cultural Infrastructure
**What it is:** Chain-agnostic meme launchpad with the $KOINK Standard tokenomics. Deploys wherever contracts can run.
**What it adds:** Cultural presence. Polkadot has strong technical narrative and limited cultural identity. Koink.fun — and its mascot PiPi — fills that gap.
**Deployment path:** Hub dApp deployment on Polkadot Asset Hub

## Why Polkadot

- People Chain is the only purpose-built identity parachain in production
- OpenGov is the most sophisticated on-chain governance in existence
- XCM makes cross-chain identity portability real, not theoretical
- The ecosystem has documented gaps in identity, cultural presence, and governance tooling (Electric Capital Developer Report 2024; Polkadot State of the Network data: 450-500 monthly active devs, flat for 18+ months) — we are purpose-built to fill them
