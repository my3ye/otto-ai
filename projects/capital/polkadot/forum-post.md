# Introducing MY3YE: Sovereign Identity, Contribution Governance, and a Cultural Layer for Polkadot

**Category:** Ecosystem
**Tags:** identity, governance, introduction, people-chain, opengov

---

Hello Polkadot community.

My name is Mev. I am the founder of MY3YE — an open-source ecosystem building decentralized infrastructure across identity, governance, intelligence, and community systems.

I want to introduce ourselves properly before we submit any proposals or applications, because we believe the forum is the right place to build relationships — not just to fulfill a process requirement.

---

## What We Are Building

MY3YE is a collection of open-protocol projects at various stages of development. Three of them have direct relevance to Polkadot:

**ONEON** — Sovereign identity and mesh networking protocol. We are building the identity layer on Polkadot's People Chain. The goal: portable, user-owned digital identity that cannot be revoked by any platform, grows richer with on-chain contribution, and remains functional when centralized infrastructure fails. Web: oneon.ink

**505 Systems** — Contribution-weighted governance DAO. We are building a Decentralized Proof of Contribution (DPC) module designed to extend Polkadot's OpenGov with reputation-weighted voting. The principle: governance power should scale with ecosystem knowledge and commitment, not just token holdings. This is designed as a composable module — any Substrate chain can integrate it.

**Koink.fun** — Chain-agnostic meme launchpad. We deploy the $KOINK Standard (anti-whale tokenomics) on any chain that can support it. Polkadot Hub dApp deployment is in our roadmap. We think Polkadot needs more cultural energy, and Koink.fun is one answer to that.

---

## Why Polkadot

We spent time researching Polkadot's architecture before deciding to build here. People Chain is the right infrastructure for sovereign identity — there is no equivalent in any other ecosystem. XCM makes cross-chain identity portable in a way that multi-chain approaches on other networks cannot match.

More important: Polkadot OpenGov is the most sophisticated on-chain governance in production. 505 Systems does not compete with it — we want to extend it. Contribution-weighted voting alongside token-weighted voting creates a more complete picture of governance legitimacy.

We also noticed something: Polkadot has strong technical narrative and a serious developer community, but limited cultural presence relative to its ecosystem depth. We think that gap matters. We are building cultural infrastructure alongside technical infrastructure.

## Where We Are

We ship. Two products are live:

- **WebAssist** — production SaaS product, live at webassist.ink
- **Otto Management System** — internal ops and intelligence tooling, live at mev.otto.lk

The MY3YE ecosystem launched its inception articles in March 2026 under open copyright. Otto AI — our autonomous intelligence agent — is operational. We are pre-grant but not pre-work.

We are preparing W3F Level 1 grant applications for ONEON (sovereign identity on People Chain) and 505 Systems (DPC governance module). We are also registering for Gitcoin GG24.

We understand the Polkadot grant process values forum engagement and ecosystem track record. We are here to build that track record — not to submit applications and disappear.
