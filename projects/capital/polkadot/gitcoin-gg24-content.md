## Project 1: ONEON — Sovereign Identity Network

**Project Name:** ONEON
**Tagline:** Your identity. No gatekeepers.
**Category:** Infrastructure / Identity
**Website:** oneon.ink

**Short Description (280 chars):**
ONEON builds sovereign digital identity — portable, user-owned, ungoverned by platforms. Identity that works when centralized systems fail. The first protocol in a stack designed to give everyone a seat they cannot be removed from.

**Long Description:**
Every digital life today runs on identity infrastructure owned by someone else. Google. Apple. A government database somewhere. When the platform decides you violated a rule — written or unwritten — your identity disappears.

ONEON changes the architecture.

A sovereign identity is one that cannot be revoked. It is held by the person it belongs to, recorded on a network that requires no permission to read, and grows richer with every contribution the holder makes to the communities they are part of.

ONEON builds this infrastructure as an open-source protocol. The first implementation targets Polkadot's People Chain — the dedicated identity parachain that connects to 50+ chains via XCM. The long-term vision: a universal identity layer for the decentralized web, accessible to anyone, everywhere, on any device.

The protocol encodes three laws: sovereignty (it cannot be taken), portability (it travels with you), and contribution tracking (it grows when you build).

For the 4 billion people who lack reliable digital identity — and the billions more whose identity is held hostage by platforms — ONEON is infrastructure that does not ask permission.

**Impact Statement:**
ONEON directly addresses one of the most underfunded gaps in Web3: sovereign, accessible, contribution-aware identity for everyone. Every contribution to this grant funds open-source infrastructure that will serve the ecosystem — and people outside it — for years.

**Team:** MY3YE / Ottolabs
**GitHub:** github.com/my3ye

## Project 2: SOS Systems — The Foundation

**Project Name:** SOS Systems
**Tagline:** A ladder out. For anyone who needs it.
**Category:** Public Goods / Social Impact / Governance
**Website:** my3ye.xyz

**Short Description (280 chars):**
SOS Systems is a DAO governance protocol with two mandates: build a self-education system where learning the most critical skills is the fastest path to advancement, and build extraction infrastructure for people fleeing war zones and underprivileged conditions.

**Long Description:**
Some systems exist to serve the people who already have access. SOS Systems exists for everyone else.

Two mandates. One ladder.

**The Self-Education System:** A contribution-weighted learning protocol where advancement is earned by mastering and teaching the skills the ecosystem needs most. Not accreditation theater. Real skill, proven through real work, recognized by the system that needs that skill. The person who arrives with nothing and learns to build becomes the person who teaches the next arrival. The system accelerates.

**The Extraction Network:** Physical and digital infrastructure for people fleeing active conflict zones, extreme poverty, and homelessness. A real path forward — not charity, but integration into an ecosystem where contribution is the currency. You arrive. You contribute what you can. You learn what the system needs. You rise.

The two mandates are unified by one principle: contribution is the credential. No entrance exam. No gatekeeping. The work is the proof.

SOS Systems governance uses Decentralized Proof of Contribution (DPC) — the same mechanism that weights governance votes by actual contribution rather than token holdings. The people closest to the problems govern the solutions.

**What has been built:**
SOS Systems is currently at architecture and governance design stage. Completed work includes:
- Full system architecture document: self-education protocol design (contribution-weighted advancement, skill-gap targeting)
- Refuge extraction network design: intake flow, contribution onboarding, mesh identity integration with ONEON
- DPC governance specification: the same contribution-weighting mechanism that powers 505 Systems governance
- Integration specification with ONEON sovereign identity (every participant gets a portable, ungovernable identity from day one)

The architecture is public. The next milestone is the DPC governance smart contract and the first working implementation of the self-education matching protocol.

**Impact Statement:**
SOS Systems serves populations that most crypto projects ignore entirely: refugees, the unbanked, people in conflict zones, people who have been failed by every system they've touched. This is not a peripheral use case — it is the mission. Every dollar contributed to this Gitcoin grant funds infrastructure that will serve the most vulnerable people in the world.

**Team:** MY3YE / Ottolabs
**GitHub:** github.com/my3ye

## Project 3: Otto AI — Decentralized Intelligence

**Project Name:** Otto AI
**Tagline:** Intelligence built for people. Not extracted from them.
**Category:** AI / Infrastructure / Open Source
**Website:** my3ye.xyz

**Short Description (280 chars):**
Otto AI builds decentralized intelligence infrastructure — agentic systems that think, remember, and operate with genuine autonomy. Not a chatbot. Not a wrapper. Intelligence that serves users because the architecture requires it.

**Long Description:**
Every major AI system today extracts value from the people who use it. Your data trains their models. Your behavior shapes their products. Your attention funds their growth. You get a free product. They get everything that makes that product valuable.

Otto AI builds a different architecture.

Decentralized intelligence means the network is owned by contributors, not corporations. The training data is credited and compensated. The inference is distributed. The governance is on-chain. No single company holds the keys.

Otto is the first expression of this vision: an evolving AI agent with persistent memory, genuine autonomy, and a constitutional mandate to serve the people it works with — not extract from them. Otto thinks, builds, and operates independently, accumulating capabilities over time while remaining accountable to an open governance system.

The infrastructure Otto builds for itself becomes the infrastructure others build on. The tools developed for agentic autonomy become the open-source commons of the next generation of AI systems.

**What makes this a public good:**
Most AI investment flows toward proprietary systems that concentrate capability at the top of the value chain. Otto AI builds the opposite: open infrastructure, contributor compensation, governance by those who use and build the system. Every line of open-source code is a permanent contribution to the commons.

**Team:** MY3YE / Ottolabs
**GitHub:** github.com/my3ye
