## Executive Summary

As of March 2026, the grant landscape has shifted significantly. Several programs have restructured or closed (W3F general grants, Gitcoin Grants Stack), while new AI-specific programs have emerged (Arbitrum Trailblazer, ASI Alliance, Solana AI fund). The highest-immediate-value opportunities for MY3YE are: **(1) GG25 registration now**, **(2) Ethereum Foundation ESP Wishlist**, **(3) Starknet Seed/Growth Grants**, **(4) Solana Foundation standard grants**, and **(5) ENS Public Goods Builder Grants**. The W3F general grants program has been discontinued — the Polkadot Treasury (OpenGov) is now the primary path.

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|

| **Application complexity** | Medium–High |

**What changed:** The EF paused its open grants program in early 2025 to reduce burn rate and redesign. In November 2025, it relaunched with a new two-pathway structure:

1. **Wishlist**: Broad priority areas (cryptography, privacy, application layer, security, community growth). Projects propose how they address a listed priority.

**Requirements:** Technical soundness, ecosystem impact, open-source outputs, cost-effectiveness. Non-commercial focus (for-profit companies may apply if grant work is open-source).

### 6.2 Polkadot OpenGov Treasury (PRIMARY PATH)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Status** | OPEN (continuous, on-chain governance) |
| **Amount** | Small spends: a few thousand DOT. Large proposals: no formal cap (voted by DOT holders). Available in DOT, USDC, USDT |
| **Application complexity** | High |
| **Timeline** | Proposal → forum discussion → on-chain referendum → voting period → enactment |
| **Apply** | polkassembly.io — create Treasury Proposal via Polkassembly |

**Process:**
1. Post on Polkadot Forum for community feedback (mandatory for Treasury proposals)
2. Submit on-chain treasury proposal via Polkassembly
3. Referendum assigned to treasury track based on amount
4. DOT holders vote during approval period
5. Enactment on passage

**Track spending limits:** Multiple tracks with different limits and approval thresholds. Small Treasury (~<10K DOT) tracks have lower quorum requirements.

**MY3YE fit:** MEDIUM. ONEON (sovereign identity on Polkadot People Chain) is the strongest fit. Requires building Polkadot-native deliverables. Community support (DOT holders who will vote) is key — harder to win without existing ecosystem presence. EasyA/Polkadot community engagement (our prior work) helps here.

**Note:** The prior W3F Grants Program was the easier path for small amounts. Now that it's closed, OpenGov Treasury is the only Polkadot pathway but is more complex.
