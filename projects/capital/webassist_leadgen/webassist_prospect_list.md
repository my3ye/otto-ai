## QUALIFICATION SCORING MATRIX

Use this to prioritize outreach:

| Signal | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Currently on Wix/Squarespace (easiest upgrade) | +3 |
| No dedicated dev team | +3 |
| Site visibly dated (>18 months old) | +2 |
| Raised funding recently (credibility + budget) | +2 |
| Marketing team separated from dev team | +2 |
| Zero or near-zero SEO traffic | +2 |
| Active on LinkedIn (founder posts regularly) | +1 |
| Competitor has clearly better site | +1 |

**Priority tier:**
- 12–16 points → Top priority (outreach within 24h)
- 8–11 points → Strong prospect (outreach within 1 week)
- 5–7 points → Nurture (add to LinkedIn, engage content first)
