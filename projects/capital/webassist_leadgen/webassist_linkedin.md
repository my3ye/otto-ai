## ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY (before connecting)

Before sending a connection request, do at least one of:

1. **Like their recent post** — shows you actually looked at their profile
2. **Leave a genuine comment** — one sentence, add value, don't pitch
3. **Reference something specific** in the connection note ("Saw your post about [X]...")

This warms the connection significantly. Cold connections with no prior engagement convert at ~15%. Warmed connections convert at ~40%+.

## CONVERSATION ESCALATION SCRIPTS

**If they reply "Tell me more":**
> Great. [COMPANY] is the kind of team WebAssist is built for. I'll send you a short overview — and if it resonates, let's book 20 minutes. What's your email?

**If they reply "We handle our site internally":**
> Completely get it. Most teams we work with said the same thing before we started — usually means someone's spending 3-5 hours a week on it that could go elsewhere. If that changes, keep us in mind.

**If they reply "Not the right time":**
> No worries at all. Best of luck with [COMPANY] — following your progress. Feel free to reach out when timing shifts.

**If they ask about pricing:**
> Three plans: Basic at $99/mo, Pro at $299/mo, Business at $499/mo. All month-to-month, no setup fee. Most early-stage teams land on Pro. Want me to break down what each covers?
