## PROGRAM MECHANICS (Internal Reference)

**Referral tracking:**
- Unique referral links generated per customer at account creation
- Links persist indefinitely (no expiry)
- Attribution: last-click, 30-day cookie window

**Credit processing:**
- Automatic trigger: referred user's first payment clears
- Credit applied: within 48h to referrer's account
- Credit type: account balance (applies to next invoice automatically)

**Edge cases:**
- Referrer churns before credit applies → credit held for 60 days, then forfeited
- Referred user refunds → credit reversed
- Referrer refers themselves via second account → flagged, both accounts reviewed

**Promotion channels:**
- Post-payment confirmation email (Day 1)
- In-dashboard banner (persistent)
- Monthly newsletter mention
- Direct outreach email (30 days after signup)
