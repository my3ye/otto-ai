## SEQUENCE NOTES

**Sending rules:**
- Send from a personal domain email, not @webassist.ink (too corporate on cold)
- Use first-name only, no "Dear" or "Hi there"
- Do NOT send all 3 on the same week. Space is crucial for credibility.
- If they reply at any point — pull them out of the sequence immediately

**Tracking:**
- Use link tracking on the calendar link only (no open-pixel — looks spammy)
- Log replies in lead CRM within 24h

**If they reply "not interested":**
> "Totally fair. If timing changes or you hear of someone who needs it, I'd appreciate the intro. Good luck with [COMPANY]."

**If they reply "send more info":**
> Send the one-pager PDF (webassist.ink/assets/one-pager.pdf — to be created), then follow up in 3 days.

**If they reply "what's the catch":**
> "No catch — no setup fee, no annual contract. Month-to-month. If we don't make your site better in 30 days, you cancel. That's the offer."
