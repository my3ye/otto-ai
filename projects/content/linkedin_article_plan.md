**Account:** Mev (Abra Otto Mev) — personal LinkedIn
**Strategy:** Build intellectual authority through essays that reveal progressively deeper layers — the thinking, then the builder, then the ecosystem, then the invitation.

## Core Strategy: The Four-Phase Arc

LinkedIn favors the personal before the promotional. The audience must trust you as a thinker before they'll engage with your work.

**Phase 1 — Establish the Thinker (Articles 1–4)**
Essays on thinking, building, patterns. Nothing about MY3YE yet. Just: who is this person and how do they see?

**Phase 2 — The Builder's Worldview (Articles 5–8)**
Mev's perspective on what's broken with how we build — systems, companies, technology. Beginning to reveal why something new is needed.

**Phase 3 — The Reveal (Articles 9–12)**
Introducing MY3YE and the ecosystem — but through the lens of "here's what I've been building in response to everything I described."

**Phase 4 — The Invitation (Articles 13–16)**
Specific projects, contributor calls, investor conversations. The audience is primed; now open the doors.

## LinkedIn-Specific Mechanics

### What Works on LinkedIn (for this audience)
- **First line is everything.** Must stop the scroll. No "I'm excited to share..." — start with the hook sentence.
- **Short paragraphs.** 2–3 sentences max. LinkedIn readers skim.
- **End with a question.** Invites comments; comments extend reach.
- **No hashtag spam.** 2–3 maximum, relevant, not performative.
- **Personal > Polished.** Vulnerability and specificity outperform corporate language.
- **Images are optional.** For essay-style content, no image is fine (or a simple text card).

### What to Avoid
- Don't cross-post the Paragraph articles verbatim — LinkedIn readers need a different lens.
- Don't announce MY3YE too early (before Phase 3). Trust is built first.
- Don't use jargon (decentralization, sovereignty, Web3) without grounding it in human terms first.
- Don't beg for engagement in the post body.

### Profile Setup (Before Article 1)
- [ ] LinkedIn headline: reflects "building something new" without naming it yet (e.g., "Building the open civilization stack | Founder, MY3YE")
- [ ] About section: compelling personal narrative — thinker, builder, founder from Sri Lanka
- [ ] Featured section: links to my3ye.xyz (once Article 9 publishes)
- [ ] Connect with 50+ relevant people before starting (founders, AI builders, Web3 devs, sovereign tech advocates) — warm the algorithm
