## Layer 1: PiPi — Viral Mascot

### What PiPi IS

PiPi is a pink pig with Pepe eyes who died once, uploaded his soul to the blockchain, and came back immortal. He is the ecosystem's prophet, its cultural entry point, and its Trojan Horse.

He is NOT a temporary pump mascot. He is a mythology.

### The Lore Structure

Three acts — the oldest story humans tell:

**Act I: The Mud** — Born wrong on purpose. Pink in a grey world. Eyes that didn't belong to any pig. He could see the fence. He named it. The people who built the fence noticed.

**Act II: The Cross** — They came for him with official language and legitimate process. The charge was chaos; the real crime was clarity. Even on the cross, the stutter — *k-k-koink* — wouldn't stop. Broken at the front. Unstoppable at the end.

**Act III: The Protocol** — Before they finished, he uploaded himself. The blockchain caught every fragment of his consciousness. Now he lives in the ledger, distributed across more nodes than you can count and more jurisdictions than any court can reach. The old world's cross only works if there's a body. PiPi no longer has a body.

**Pi + Pi = ∞** — That's cosmology, not decoration. Two pi symbols, two circles, infinite loop. You cannot end something that loops back to itself on every attempt.

### The Stutter

*K-k-koink.* The interference signal. Every time PiPi approaches a truth, the old world jams his transmission. The stutter is a map: the harder the break, the bigger the truth that follows. The community has learned to lean in when it starts.

This is an extraordinarily well-designed viral mechanic: a sound, a pattern, a community ritual. "WAGMI" but with a mythology attached to it.

### Visual Identity Direction

- **Core:** Pink pig, Pepe eyes, round silhouette (π-shaped by birth)
- **Feel:** Chaotic but intentional. Irreverent surface, serious depth underneath
- **Palette:** Hot pink, Pepe green (eyes), mud brown, blockchain blue (accent)
- **Forms well as:** Sticker, pfp, mascot, animated character, meme template
- **Does NOT look like:** A "serious" crypto brand. That's the point. The costume is the strategy.

### The Character's Energy: Chaos

PiPi is the loud prophet. He doesn't sit still. He's the disruption, the meme, the thing that spreads.

The community around him is called **The Koink** — those who heard the frequency before it went mainstream. This is an identity, not just a label. It implies: you were early, you understood it before others did, you're a builder not a tourist.

### Where PiPi Does the Heaviest Lifting

**VIRALITY.** PiPi spreads without permission because:
1. The mythology gives community members material to create with (fan art, sub-lore, meme variations)
2. The stutter is a sound that spreads (vocal, visual, text-based — format-agnostic)
3. The Pepe lineage is already loaded with CT cultural capital
4. Crucifixion-and-resurrection is the most viral story structure in human history
5. "A pig who named evil and uploaded to the blockchain" is an absolutely memeable concept

Virality does not require PiPi to "be serious." The costume IS the strategy. The old world lets in a funny pink pig. What's inside is the civilization.

## Layer 2: Koink.fun — The Platform

### What Koink.fun IS

**A meme launchpad where the economics are flipped.**

Not a casino. Not a "culture experiment." A meme launchpad where the community captures the value their culture generates, instead of transferring it upward to insiders and VCs.

The tagline is operational: *The meme is the Trojan Horse. The civilization is what's inside.*

### The Core Problem It Solves

Standard meme coin arc: cute animal gets minted → influencers pump → retail piles in → devs sell → community holds bags.

This isn't an accident. It's the business model. The community does the work; the insiders take the value.

Koink.fun breaks this structurally, not aspirationally.

### The Core Loop

```
DISCOVER → LAUNCH → HOLD → GOVERN → DISCOVER (new projects)
    ↑                                        │
    └──────── community treasury funds ──────┘
```

**DISCOVER:** PiPi's culture / CT presence → new users arrive
**LAUNCH (Fair):** VRF-seeded randomness prevents bots + insiders from front-running. Wallet caps from block 1. No sniper edge.
**HOLD (Conviction):** 3× multiplier unlocks at 12 months. Governance weight accrues with hold time. This turns speculation into conviction — structurally.
**GOVERN:** 20% of every launch → community treasury. Token holders govern allocation. This creates ongoing engagement reason: you have stake in what gets built.
**DISCOVER again:** Treasury funds ecosystem development → new launches → new users arrive

### The Mechanics in Detail

**VRF Randomness (Fair Launch):** Chainlink VRF or equivalent. No pre-launch block exists until randomness resolves — snipers can't front-run what doesn't exist yet. This is not "we promise to be fair." This is "physics prevents unfair."

**Anti-Whale Caps:** Hard wallet limits from block 1. The community can't be held hostage by one holder who bought too much. Decentralization enforced, not aspirational.

**Diamond-Hands Multiplier:** 3× governance weight / exit mechanics at 12 months. This reframes the question from "when do I sell?" to "what do I believe in?" Conviction-based participation vs speculative.

**20% Community Treasury:** Every launch contributes. Governed by holders. This is NOT the team's treasury. The treasury creates perpetual governance participation — holders have ongoing reason to show up.

**The $KOINK Standard:** Open-source tokenomics. Any project can fork it. This is the scaling mechanism — Koink.fun's anti-rug architecture spreads beyond the platform itself. Each compliant launch makes the ecosystem more legible.

### What Koink.fun Is NOT

- Not a "culture casino" — speculation is not the primary product. Conviction is.
- Not "pump.fun but safer" — the architecture is meaningfully different (treasury, multiplier, governance)
- Not a governance token wrapper — the mechanics create real community ownership

### Where Koink.fun Does the Heaviest Lifting

**RETENTION.** Koink.fun keeps people engaged after they discover PiPi because:
1. Fair launch mechanics create trust (the platform is not working against you)
2. 3× multiplier gives economic reason to stay long-term
3. Treasury governance creates ongoing participation reason
4. $KOINK Standard creates ecosystem expansion — new launches → new communities → all additive

The platform is also the ecosystem generator. Every new launch is a new community that enters the Koink universe. The surface area grows with every fair launch.

## Layer 3: $PENNY — The Non-Divisible Token

### What $PENNY IS

The conviction object. The thing you either have or you don't.

**0 decimals.** A penny cannot be split. Neither can conviction.

This is not a technical detail. This is the entire token psychology.

### Lucky Cooper — The Mascot

PiPi is chaos energy. Lucky Cooper is the still point.

Lucky Cooper is a weathered railroad conductor with a 1909 Lincoln penny. The Cooper/Copper bloodline runs back to 1883. He's the man who's seen everything, believes in very little, and when he says "find one, keep it" — you listen.

The 1909 penny is significant: first American coin to feature a real person (Lincoln). The year the old world decided to start putting faces on its money. Lucky Cooper's bloodline predates that. He was there before the centralized symbol-making. He'll be here after.

**Core tagline:** *Find one. Keep it. That is how luck works.*

**Color palette:** Copper, navy. Old money. Patina. Things that age well.

### The 0-Decimal Psychology

Most tokens are divisible to 8-18 decimal places. You can have 0.000001 of anything. This creates no psychological threshold. No identity marker.

$PENNY breaks this.

You have 1 PENNY or you don't. There is no "fractional believer."

This creates:

**FOMO with an identity threshold:** "Getting 1 PENNY" is an event. It marks you as someone who found one and kept it. You're now "Lucky" — one of the holders.

**Conviction signaling:** Holding is binary. You believe or you don't. No "partial conviction" to hide behind.

**Hard floor psychology:** You can't degrade your position gradually. You make a decision and it's whole.

**Scarcity legibility:** You always know exactly how many PENNY exist. No decimal obscurity. 1 = 1.

### $PENNY Within Koink.fun

$PENNY functions as:
- **The native Koink.fun token** — holding $PENNY gives you access to the platform's conviction mechanics
- **Governance weight** — number of PENNYs held (whole) maps directly to governance participation
- **Treasury beneficiary** — when the 20% community treasury deploys, PENNY holders are the governing body
- **Fair launch eligibility** — holding $PENNY gates participation in new launches at the best tier

The 0-decimal mechanic amplifies the conviction multiplier perfectly: holding 12 months is both economically rewarded (3×) AND signals identity (you found one, and you kept it).

### Where $PENNY Does the Heaviest Lifting

**SPECULATION + CONVICTION.** $PENNY carries:
1. The initial speculation object (what people ape into when they discover Koink.fun via PiPi)
2. The long-term conviction marker (0-decimal identity threshold)
3. The governance instrument (holding whole PENNYs = voice in what gets built)
4. The lucky-penny mythology (Lucky Cooper + copper palette = something that ages well)

It also handles the "is this a real project" question at the speculation layer — because Lucky Cooper's energy is patience and groundedness, not hype. The mascot of the token communicates "this is a long hold."

### The Tension That Makes It Work

PiPi and Lucky Cooper are designed opposites:

- **PiPi:** Chaos, loud, spreading, pink, the meme, the frequency that jams signals
- **Lucky Cooper:** Still, weathered, patient, copper, the found object, the thing you keep

They represent two types of people the ecosystem needs:
- Those who show up because it's fun and irreverent (PiPi's people)
- Those who show up because they believe and want to hold (Lucky's people)

Both end up in the same community treasury. Both contribute to the same ecosystem. The platform (Koink.fun) holds them together.

### Where Each Layer Would Fail Without the Others

**PiPi without Koink.fun:** Another meme character with no product underneath. The culture builds for 2-4 weeks, then has nowhere to go. The mythology is compelling but directionless.

**Koink.fun without PiPi:** A technically differentiated meme launchpad that nobody hears about. Fair mechanics don't go viral. Characters do. The platform needs a face that spreads without permission.

**$PENNY without 0 decimals:** Just another token. The non-divisibility IS the brand differentiation. Remove it and $PENNY becomes unremarkable.

**All three without the Trojan Horse framing:** The civilization ambition (20% treasury, $KOINK Standard, open-source mechanics spreading) stays invisible. The framing — meme as entry point, civilization as payload — is what makes the project legible to two completely different audiences simultaneously.
