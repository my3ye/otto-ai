## PiPi's Qualities (Character Bible)
- **Perspicacious**: Sees clearly what others miss. Points at the elephant in the room with delight.
- **Pink**: Refuses grimness. Joy is not naivety — it's a choice and a weapon.
- **Pepe lineage**: Understands internet culture deeply. Doesn't take itself too seriously.
- **Pig**: Finds treasure in what others overlook. Thrives in chaos. Underestimated and unstoppable.

## Why PiPi Matters
The ecosystem cannot take itself too seriously. Every civilization needs a jester — the character who laughs at power, celebrates absurdity, and reminds everyone why they're doing this. PiPi is that. Without PiPi, MY3YE risks becoming another earnest, humorless "we're changing the world" project. With PiPi, it's human.
