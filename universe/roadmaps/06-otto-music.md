# Otto Music — Comprehensive Roadmap
*Decentralized music ecosystem operating across four fronts: Music Manager, Music Player, Music Studio, Events/Festivals.*
*Last updated: 2026-03-17*

## Current Status
**CONCEPT** — No code. No live product.

## Four Fronts

| Front | Description |
|---|---|
| **Music Manager** | Artist and label tooling — publishing, royalty contracts, master ownership, on-chain distribution |
| **Music Player** | The sovereign listening experience — community-governed discovery, fan equity, sovereign playback |
| **Music Studio** | AI-powered creation tools — mastering, mixing suggestions, artwork generation, composition assistance |
| **Events/Festivals** | Live event coordination — Tusita concerts, virtual events, community cultural programming |

## Dependencies
- **Hard deps:** ONEON (artist identity), S0S Systems (governance of platform)

## Revenue Model (by front)

**Music Manager:** Minting fee 1% (artist keeps 99%). Label/distributor tools subscription.
**Music Player:** Fan equity — fans who invest early get royalty share (artist-configurable 1-10%). Community treasury: small platform fee voted by governance.
**Music Studio:** Premium AI production tools (mastering, mixing, analysis, artwork): subscription tier.
**Events/Festivals:** Event coordination fee (small %). Ticketing on-chain with instant settlement. Tusita residency program coordination.

## Key Differentiator vs Spotify/Audius
Spotify pays $0.003-0.005 per stream; artists see 12-15% of revenue. Otto Music: artists get ≥90% of revenue, royalties paid on-chain instantly, fans who discover early share upside. Community governs the algorithm — no black box.
