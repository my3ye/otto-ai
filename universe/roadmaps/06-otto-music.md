## Revenue Model
- Minting fee: 1% (artist keeps 99%)
- Fan equity: fans who invest early get royalty share (artist-configurable 1-10%)
- Premium artist tools (AI mixing, mastering, analysis): subscription
- Community treasury: small platform fee voted by governance
- Live events: coordination platform for Tusita concerts + virtual events

## Key Differentiator vs Spotify/Audius
Spotify pays $0.003-0.005 per stream; artists see 12-15% of revenue. Otto Music: artists get ≥90% of revenue, royalties paid on-chain instantly, fans who discover early share upside. Community governs the algorithm — no black box.
