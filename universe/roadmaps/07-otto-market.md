# Otto Market — Comprehensive Roadmap
*Decentralized commerce. Zero extraction. Community-governed marketplace.*
*Last updated: 2026-03-16*

## Current Status
**CONCEPT** — Community waitlist referenced on otto.lk. No code.

## Dependencies
- **Hard deps:** ONEON (buyer/seller identity), S0S Systems (governance of platform rules)
- **Soft deps:** Koink/KOIN (payment token), Ottolabs (physical goods supply), All MY3YE projects (digital goods)
- **Enables:** Every project selling its outputs

---

## Phase 1 — Digital Goods MVP (NOW → 60 days)
**Goal:** First store. First sale. Zero physical logistics complexity.

### Milestones
1. **MVP scope locked** — Digital goods only: music, art, templates, courses, software
2. **Commerce protocol spec** — Storefront NFT standard, listing format, payment flow
3. **Architecture research** — Learn from OpenSea, Origin, WooCommerce-on-chain
4. **Community waitlist live** — 500+ signups on otto.lk
5. **First 5 seller onboarded** — From MY3YE community: artists, designers, builders

### Success Criteria
- Protocol spec documented and Mev-reviewed
- 5 sellers ready with digital product listings
- Waitlist ≥500

---

## Phase 2 — Alpha Marketplace (60 days → 4 months)
**Goal:** Real sales happening. Community using it.

### Milestones
1. **Marketplace alpha live** — Search, list, buy. ONEON auth for sellers.
2. **Smart contract payments** — Crypto payment (Solana/USDC), instant settlement
3. **Digital delivery** — Automated delivery of digital goods after payment
4. **MY3YE goods first** — Otto Music tracks, art from Tusita artists, Otto templates listed
5. **Review/reputation system** — Buyer/seller trust layer
6. **1,000 transactions completed**

### Success Criteria
- 1,000 completed transactions
- 0 failed deliveries
- 0 payment disputes unresolved
- $50,000+ total GMV

## Phase 3 — Physical Goods (4→12 months)
**Goal:** Physical goods from Ottolabs and community sellers.

### Milestones
1. **Physical goods support** — Shipping address collection, order tracking
2. **Ottolabs goods listed** — Otto Puck pre-orders, community-manufactured goods
3. **Tusita produce** — Agricultural outputs from Tusita farms available
4. **Open seller program** — Any community member can open a store
5. **S0S governance live** — Platform fee %, content policies, dispute resolution voted on-chain
6. **10,000+ active sellers**

### Success Criteria
- 10,000 sellers active
- Physical goods fulfillment rate ≥97%
- First Ottolabs product sold through Otto Market
- $1M+ GMV

---

## Phase 4 — Civilization Commerce Layer (12→24 months)
**Goal:** Otto Market is the commerce infrastructure of the ecosystem. Everything produced by the civilization, available to the world.

### Milestones
1. **$OTTO rewards** — Buyers earn governance weight for purchases, sellers earn for reliability
2. **Multi-currency** — USDC, $KOINK, local currency via on/off-ramp
3. **Zero-commission model** — Platform costs covered by community treasury (fees voted to 0%)
4. **Cross-ecosystem integration** — Otto Properties lists rentals, Otto Travel lists experiences, Otto Music sells merch
5. **Global shipping partnerships** — Integration with shipping networks for affordable physical delivery

### Success Criteria
- $10M+ annual GMV
- Platform fees voted to near-zero by community
- All major MY3YE project outputs available on Otto Market
- Recognized as viable alternative to Amazon/eBay for community commerce

---

## Revenue Model (transitioning to community treasury)
- Phase 1-2: 5% platform fee (covers development costs)
- Phase 3: 2.5% community-voted fee
- Phase 4: Fee voted by community, surplus to treasury
- Premium seller tools: analytics, promotion, featured listings

## Key Differentiator vs Amazon/Etsy
Amazon/Etsy extract 15-40% from sellers. Otto Market: community votes the fee (target: near-zero), surplus returned to contributors. Sellers govern the platform they sell on.
