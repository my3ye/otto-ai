# Otto Travel — Comprehensive Roadmap
*Community-governed global travel. Tusita as the first destination.*
*Last updated: 2026-03-16*

## Current Status
**CONCEPT** — No code. No live product. Roadmap builds on Tusita progress.

## Dependencies
- **Hard deps:** ONEON (traveler identity + payments), Tusita (first destination)
- **Soft deps:** Otto Properties (property listings), S0S Systems (governance of platform fees)
- **Blocks:** Tusita income generation (Otto Travel is how Tusita makes money from visitors)

---

## Phase 1 — Experience Ceylon MVP (NOW → 60 days)
**Goal:** First 10 hosts. First traveler booking.

### Milestones
1. **Waitlist launched** — 200+ hosts and travelers from Sri Lanka focus area
2. **Smart contract escrow designed** — Payment flow: traveler pays → escrow → release on check-out
3. **Property NFT standard** — How properties are listed and governed on-chain
4. **Legal research done** — Short-term rental regulations + crypto payment legality in Sri Lanka
5. **First 10 hosts onboarded** — Especially Tusita-adjacent properties

### Success Criteria
- 200+ waitlist signups
- Smart contract design reviewed by legal (or Mev decision made to proceed anyway)
- 10 verified hosts with live listings

**Goal:** Real travelers booking through Otto Travel. Real money flowing.

5. **100+ bookings completed**

- 100 completed bookings
- 0 payment disputes unresolved

## Phase 4 — Parallel Civilization Travel Layer (12→24 months)
**Goal:** Otto Travel is how the world visits the MY3YE civilization. Every Tusita location, every community property, every experience — discoverable and bookable.

### Milestones
1. **All Tusita locations** — 3+ locations fully integrated
2. **$OTTO rewards** — Travelers earn governance weight for verified stays

### Success Criteria
- 100,000+ bookings/year
- Operating profitably on community treasury model
- Featured in major travel publication as "decentralized alternative to Airbnb"

---

## Revenue Model
- Platform fee: 5% (community-voted, lower than Airbnb's 15%)
- Premium listing tiers for hosts
- Otto Experiences: curated packages at higher margin
- Community treasury: surplus distributed to governance participants

## Key Differentiator vs Airbnb
Airbnb extracts 15-25% from hosts and guests. Otto Travel operates on community treasury — platform fee voted by community, surplus returned to contributors. The hosts govern the platform they use.
