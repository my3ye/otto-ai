# Ottolabs — Comprehensive Roadmap
*Physical means of production. Collectively owned hardware, robotics, factories, farms, energy.*
*Last updated: 2026-03-16*

## Current Status
**CONCEPT** — Defined in universe YAML. No hardware prototyped. No factory.

## Dependencies
- **Hard deps:** S0S Systems (DAO governance for factory output)
- **Soft deps:** ONEON (device identity), Koink/KOIN (community treasury funding)
- **Blocks:** Tusita physical infrastructure, Otto Properties construction, Panik mesh, Shakrah (Otto Band)

---

## Phase 1 — Otto Puck Prototype (NOW → 90 days)
**Goal:** First Ottolabs device exists in the real world.

### Milestones
1. **Puck hardware spec finalized** — SBC choice (Raspberry Pi CM4 or similar), enclosure dimensions, connectivity (WiFi/BT/Ethernet), power (USB-C + optional battery)
2. **BOM costed** — Bill of materials for 100 units. Target <$50/unit for accessibility.
3. **ODM contact made** — Manufacturing partner identified in Sri Lanka or SE Asia
4. **Puck software stack** — OS, Otto agent version, ONEON integration documented
5. **10 prototype units** — Built, tested, running Otto agent + ONEON auth
6. **Crowdfunding model designed** — Pre-order mechanism, pricing, governance token for early backers

### Success Criteria
- 10 functional Puck prototypes
- Otto agent running on Puck hardware
- ONEON identity auth working on Puck
- BOM cost ≤$75/unit (prototype), target ≤$50/unit (production)
- Pre-order campaign design approved by Mev

## Phase 2 — Puck v1.0 Production (90 days → 6 months)
**Goal:** 1,000 units shipped to contributors and buyers.

### Milestones
1. **Manufacturing partner locked** — ODM confirmed, production timeline committed
2. **Pre-order campaign launched** — Crowdfunding/pre-order live, targeting 1,000 units minimum
3. **1,000 Puck units shipped**
4. **Compute contribution live** — Puck owners contribute compute to distributed Otto network
5. **Governance weight earned** — Running a Puck earns DPC in S0S governance
6. **Panik mesh v0** — Puck devices contributing to Panik ONEON mesh

### Success Criteria
- 1,000 Puck units shipped to contributors
- ≥800 Pucks contributing compute to the network
- $500K+ crowdfunding raised (funds next product)
- Compute contribution measurable and rewarded

## Phase 3 — Device Ecosystem (6→18 months)
**Goal:** Full device family. First Tusita factory space.

### Milestones
1. **Otto Home** — Residential node: home intelligence hub, Tusita mesh node, energy management
2. **Otto Band v1** — Biometric wearable: heart rate, sleep, activity — Shakrah integration
3. **Robot Tier 1 design contracted** — Humanoid worker spec, manufacturing partner identified
4. **First factory space** — Tusita community location: workshop for Ottolabs production
5. **Energy integration** — Solar array at Tusita factory; first self-powered Ottolabs facility
6. **S0S factory governance** — Community votes on what gets made, in what quantities, at what price

### Success Criteria
- Otto Home v1 shipped to Tusita Islanders
- Otto Band v1 shipped and integrated with Shakrah
- Factory space operational at Tusita
- Factory governance decisions going through S0S
- Robot Tier 1 prototype contracts signed

---

## Phase 4 — Collective Means of Production (18 months → 3 years)
**Goal:** Ottolabs is the physically productive layer of the civilization. Collective ownership of machines.

### Milestones
1. **Robot Tier 1 deployed** — Humanoid workers at Tusita construction and operations
2. **Vertical farming live** — Ottolabs agricultural system producing food for Tusita communities
3. **Custom silicon design begun** — Puck v2 with custom chip (reduces cost, increases capability)
4. **Ottolabs phone** — ONEON-native mobile device
5. **Energy sovereignty** — Tusita locations ≥80% renewable (solar + wind + storage)
6. **$10M+ in collectively-owned assets**

### Success Criteria
- Robots contributing to Tusita operations (measurable productivity)
- Food production covering ≥30% of Tusita community needs
- Ottolabs assets collectively owned by 10,000+ contributors
- $10M+ in collectively-owned productive infrastructure
