# Tusita — Comprehensive Roadmap
*Parallel civilization. Sovereign physical communities. The destination.*
*Last updated: 2026-03-16*

## Current Status
**CONCEPT** — tusita.xyz live with landing page. No physical location yet.

## Dependencies
- **Hard deps:** ONEON (identity/governance for Islanders), S0S Systems (DAO governance), Ottolabs (device mesh, construction robots)
- **Soft deps:** Shakrah (wellness program), Otto AI (intelligent community ops)
- **Blocks:** Otto Travel, Otto Properties (flagship locations)

## Phase 1 — Community Before Land (NOW → 60 days)
**Goal:** 500 founding Islanders identified before first location is confirmed.

### Milestones
1. **Islander application live** — Form on tusita.xyz, backend stores applications in Postgres
2. **Governance model published** — Islander Charter: rights, responsibilities, contribution tiers (Visitor/Islander/Steward)
3. **Waitlist seeded** — 500 applications from MY3YE community + Web3 audience
4. **Location scouting begun** — Sri Lanka focus: legal research on lease structures, first site visits
5. **$TUSITA token design** — Allocation, distribution, governance weight model documented

### Success Criteria
- ≥500 Islander applications received
- Governance charter published and community-reviewed
- ≥3 candidate locations identified for Phase 2

---

## Phase 2 — First Location (60 days → 6 months)
**Goal:** Physical space secured. Founding cohort invited.

### Milestones
1. **Location partnership signed** — Long-term lease or agreement for first Tusita site
2. **Founding cohort invited** — First 20-50 Islanders selected from waitlist
3. **Community charter ratified** — On-chain vote (Snapshot) by founding cohort
4. **Otto infrastructure deployed** — Wi-Fi mesh, Otto Home devices, basic compute node
5. **Shakrah wellness program** — First practitioners on-site or scheduled
6. **Otto Travel live** — First traveler bookings for Tusita experiences

### Success Criteria
- Physical location secured under favorable terms (≤$5K/month initial)
- 20+ Islanders in residence within first 60 days of opening
- On-chain governance making at least 1 real decision
- Otto mesh providing connectivity to all Islanders

---

## Phase 3 — Operations & Self-Sufficiency (6→18 months)
**Goal:** Tusita generates its own income. Fully operational parallel community.

### Milestones
1. **100+ Islanders** — Stable resident and rotating visitor population
2. **First Stewards elected** — Governance council operating
3. **Otto Travel integration** — External visitors booking through Otto Travel, income flowing
4. **Otto Properties** — First tokenized properties at Tusita (Islanders can own equity)
5. **Vertical farming pilot** — First Ottolabs agricultural system producing food on-site
6. **Energy grid** — Renewable energy (solar minimum) partially powering location
7. **Second location scouted** — Expansion plan in motion

### Success Criteria
- Location covers ≥50% of operating costs through Otto Travel bookings + Islander contributions
- First Islander who arrived penniless has built meaningful equity stake
- Community food sovereignty: ≥20% of food produced on-site
- 0 critical governance failures (contentious decisions resolved peacefully)

---

## Phase 4 — Parallel Civilization (18 months → 3 years)
**Goal:** Multiple locations. Tusita recognized as a genuine alternative way of living.

### Milestones
1. **3+ locations operational** — Sri Lanka + 1-2 international (SE Asia, Europe, Americas)
2. **Tusita passport** — Digital identity document recognized across all locations
3. **Tusita economy** — Internal economy running on ONEON identity + $TUSITA + KOIN
4. **Ottolabs manufacturing** — First small-scale factory producing Otto Puck and basic goods
5. **Education system** — S0S-powered learning program running in Tusita
6. **Media output** — Tusita as content source: Otto Music artists, documentaries, cultural exports

### Success Criteria
- 1,000+ active Islanders across all locations
- Net positive economics: revenue > operating costs across all locations
- Coverage in 5+ major media outlets as legitimate "new way of living"
- External governments or institutions recognizing Tusita governance framework
