# Shakrah — Comprehensive Roadmap
*Physical and mental wellness for the civilization. Integrated with biometric hardware.*
*Last updated: 2026-03-16*

## Current Status
**CONCEPT** — Shakrah-web landing page exists. No practitioner network yet.

- **Hard deps:** ONEON (practitioner identity verification)

- 20 verified practitioners across ≥3 modalities

## Wellness Modalities (Prioritized)
1. **Meditation & mindfulness** — Highest demand, easiest remote delivery
2. **Yoga & movement** — Video sessions work well; Tusita adds physical practice
3. **Nutrition** — Integrates with Tusita farm produce
4. **Somatic therapy** — High value, harder to scale remotely
5. **Breathwork** — Otto Band integration makes this data-rich
6. **Integrative medicine** — Long-term expansion (requires licensed practitioners)

## Privacy Model
Health data is the most sensitive data there is. Shakrah's model:
- All health data encrypted and controlled by the user (ONEON key)
- Practitioners see only what user explicitly shares
- Aggregate community health trends visible only to S0S governance
- No data sold to third parties (ever)
