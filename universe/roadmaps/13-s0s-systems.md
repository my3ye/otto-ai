## The DPC (Directed Participation Contribution) Model

Governance weight is earned, not bought. Ways to earn DPC:
- Writing code that gets merged
- Proposing and executing improvements
- Hosting/stewarding at Tusita
- Creating verified content for the ecosystem
- Teaching and onboarding new members
- Verified financial contribution

Capital can increase DPC weight up to 3x (prevents pure plutocracy). But base DPC comes from participation.

## S0S's Role in Preventing Dystopia
Most decentralized projects fail because governance is either:
1. Plutocratic (rich whale wins every vote)
2. Abandoned (no one votes, team decides anyway)

S0S solves this with DPC: contribution-weighted governance, not capital-weighted. The people building the civilization govern it. The people using it participate. The people funding it have voice but not control.
