## The Renter Equity Model

Traditional renting: 100% of rent goes to landlord. Renter builds zero equity.

Otto Properties: 80-90% rent covers costs. 10-20% automatically converts to equity tokens. After 5-7 years of renting, renter owns meaningful stake in the property. Community-governed valuation so equity is real, not paper.

## Revenue Model
- Property management fee: 5% (lower than traditional 10-15%)
- Fractional investment spreads: small spread between buy/sell
- Ottolabs construction cost savings: competitive advantage in build cost
- Community treasury: platform fees voted and surplus returned
