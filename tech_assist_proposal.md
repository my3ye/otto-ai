## What We're Building

Tech Assist is the MVP product of Assistive Technologies — an agentic AI services company for startups and SMBs. The concept: businesses get enterprise-grade AI automation without hiring a team or building it themselves.

The thesis is simple. Traditional agencies sell hours. We sell outcomes. AI handles execution; we handle strategy, design, and delivery. Same results, faster, for less.

---

## MVP Focus: Sales Automation (Start Here)

**Why Sales first, not Accounting or HR:**

- Clearest ROI. Revenue is directly measurable — clients can see the result in weeks, not months.
- Fastest adoption. 80%+ of SMB sales teams already use or want AI for outreach.
- Proven numbers: AI-powered outbound delivers 4-7x higher conversion rates, 76% increase in win rates, deals close 78% faster.
- Emotional buy-in. Founders *feel* revenue problems — easier to sell a solution to something that hurts right now.
- Accounting/HR are sticky long-term plays but have longer sales cycles and more compliance concerns.

**The Sales Assist service stack (Phase 1):**

1. **Outbound Engine** — AI-powered lead research, personalized email sequences, LinkedIn outreach, follow-up automation. Handles the volume of 3-5 human SDRs.
2. **CRM Automation** — Auto-populate CRM from calls, emails, LinkedIn. No more manual data entry.
3. **Lead Scoring** — AI qualification layer. Sales team only talks to warm leads.

## Engagement Model

**Recommended: Audit → Build → Retainer**

1. **Free AI Audit** (30-60 min call)
   - Map current sales workflow
   - Identify 2-3 highest-ROI automation candidates
   - Deliver written proposal with projected time/cost savings
   - Zero commitment — removes the "I've been burned before" objection

2. **Pilot Build** (2-4 weeks, $500-$1,500 setup)
   - Build one focused automation (outbound pipeline, lead qualification, CRM auto-update)
   - Integrate with their existing tools (WhatsApp, Google Workspace, HubSpot, etc.)
   - Handover + short training
   - First results visible within 30 days

3. **Monthly Retainer** ($300-$800/mo for Sri Lanka; $800-$1,500/mo for UAE)
   - Ongoing monitoring, optimization, new workflows
   - Quarterly KPI reviews
   - Consider 30-day satisfaction guarantee to close the first few clients

**Why this model works:**
- Free audit removes all friction from first contact
- Narrow pilot proves ROI fast before asking for retainer commitment
- LK price point ($300-$800/mo) competes directly with cost of one part-time hire
- UAE price point justified by higher market rates — same service, positioned differently
- An AI SDR at $500/mo replaces a human SDR at $30-60k/yr — this math sells itself

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | What They Do | Weakness |
|---|---|---|
| **Aiingo** (Dubai/India) | WhatsApp chatbots, CRM automation | Generic, templated — not truly agentic |
| **Vstorm** (global) | Custom AI agents, LLM apps | $50K+ minimum — priced out of SMBs |
| **Leanware** (Colombia) | AI process automation, nearshore | South American focus, not South Asia |
| **Relevance AI** (platform) | No-code AI agent builder | Tool, not service — requires internal skills |

**Our gap:** No one is doing *deeply agentic, outcome-focused services* at *SMB-accessible price points* in Sri Lanka/UAE. We're not competing with Aiingo on chatbots. We're doing full workflow transformation — and doing it cheaper than Western agencies because we're based in South Asia.
