Sri Lanka National Infrastructure Proposal architecture complete (2026-03-29). Structured as Sovereign Digital Economic Zone (SDEZ) — NOT crypto adoption.

**Key decisions:**
- SDEZ framing (IMF-compatible, government-palatable) over "crypto island"
- Port City Colombo SEZ as legal entry point (crypto licenses already issued)
- ONEON as lead entry (SL-UDI is govt's #1 digital priority, $98M budget, no tech partner)
- Joint Governance Council: 9 seats (3 SL govt + 3 MY3YE community + 2 independent + 1 diaspora)
- Capital = 0 governance weight (constitutional, prevents capture)
- $100M target for national infrastructure tier (~13% of SL annual FDI)
- Right of exit for both sides (12mo SL, 6mo MY3YE)

**Why:** IMF supervises SL recovery, explicitly blocks legal tender. El Salvador proved mandatory adoption fails. Bhutan reserve + UAE hub + SOS governance = validated hybrid. Sri Lanka has open regulatory window (CBSL drafting, new Digital Economy ministry, Port City SEZ).
