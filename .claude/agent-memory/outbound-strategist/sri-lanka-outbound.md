**Key frame:** NOT crypto — sovereign digital infrastructure + jobs + FDI. IMF-compatible.

**6 stakeholder tiers:**
1. President's Office / Ministry of Digital Economy — BOI/CPCEC entry, sandbox ask
2. Central Bank (CBSL) — fintech sandbox, AML/CFT framework input
3. Ministry of Finance — FDI + job creation + green bond framing
4. Tech Diaspora SV/London — Sanjiva Weerawarana, SLSVN, BSLCC as warm intro network
5. IMF — Resident Rep Colombo (softest entry), Digital Money team (most open ideologically)
6. Journalists — Rest of World, CoinDesk, FT EM desk; only pitch AFTER first gov meeting

**Top warm intro paths:**
- CPCEC Port City investor relations → Ministry (fastest)
- UNDP Sri Lanka → Finance Ministry (multilateral wrapper)
- Polkadot/W3F → IMF Digital Money team
- SLSVN events → diaspora + gov contacts in same room

**Templates:** A=Government, B=Diaspora, C=IMF, D=Press, E=LinkedIn connect

**Why:** Supporting SOS Systems proposal to Sri Lanka as first national implementation site. Critical window: regulatory framework in flux, $98M digital budget, post-crisis government needs wins.

**How to apply:** Use this context for any SL government outreach, diaspora engagement planning, or media pitch strategy.
