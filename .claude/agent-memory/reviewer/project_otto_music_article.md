Otto Music intro article "What the Algorithm Cannot Hear" (edcf8095, 2026-03-25, WF Step 1): MINOR_CHANGES, 7.5/10.

**Why:** Otto Music is status=concept. All product claims correctly qualified. Voice is brand-aligned. Physics framing ("This is not punishment. This is physics.") placed correctly at pivot. Four fronts structure is clean. Pre-write constraints applied well.

**Critical issues:** (1) "Listeners who find artists early and prove it on-chain share in what those artists build" appears twice with zero mechanism — one sentence of how (stake, token, NFT ticket, attestation) would make this credible rather than vaporware. (2) No CTA — same gap seen in Otto Travel, ONEON, other concept articles.

**Warnings:** Smart contracts "will enforce agreed terms automatically" overstates determinism of music rights (samples, co-writers, streaming splits are notoriously hard); no differentiation from existing Web3 music projects (Sound.xyz, Audius, Royal, Catalog); "prove it on-chain" is jargon left undefined; Tusita phrasing "being built" could imply further along than concept/early.

**How to apply:** For any content piece featuring a discovery/incentive mechanism, at least one sentence of mechanical description is required. "On-chain" alone is not sufficient — name the mechanism type.
