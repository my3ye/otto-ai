## Critical Issues (must fix before Step 2 publish)

1. **Line 28: Revenue stat still unverifiable** — "generating tens of millions in organic revenue from AI-executed tasks in a single quarter" has no source, no named protocol, no time anchor. Replacing the Bittensor $43M with a vaguer but equally uncited claim is worse for credibility — a skeptic reads it as a substitution of a specific lie with a vague one. Fix: either name 2-3 real protocols (Bittensor, Virtuals, Fetch.ai) without dollar figures, OR frame as "Early evidence from protocol networks suggests…", OR remove the qualifier "tens of millions" entirely.

2. **Line 61: "AI operating system" overclaim** — 12th recurrence of the DPC/system overclaim pattern across this content library. Otto is an agentic system, not an OS. "Operating system" implies hardware abstraction and resource management at the kernel level. Fix: "AI agent system" or "autonomous agent infrastructure."
