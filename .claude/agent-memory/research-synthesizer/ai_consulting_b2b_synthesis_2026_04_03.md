## Key Insights (ranked by confidence × actionability)

1. **$11–14B market (2026) → $90–116B by 2035, 26% CAGR** — AI consulting is the fastest-growing professional services category. Largest enterprise segment (500–999 employees) growing at 27.9% CAGR. — Confidence: HIGH | Sources: 4 (FutureMarketInsights, BusinessResearchInsights, market.us, colorwhistle.com)

2. **90% of AI projects failed to meet expectations** — Dominant buyer emotion is burned trust. Positioning against failure (not for AI) wins credibility with decision-makers who have been burned. — Confidence: HIGH | Sources: 3 (CorporateVisions, Forrester, lakeb2b.com)

3. **CFO gate on AI spend is real and growing** — Forrester confirms 25% of planned 2026 AI spend deferred to 2027. CFOs demand hard ROI metrics. Consultants who speak CFO language (payback periods, operational savings) have structural competitive advantage. — Confidence: HIGH | Sources: 2 (Forrester, CorporateVisions)

4. **SMB/startup market is structurally underserved** — Big 5 (Accenture ~$3B GenAI bookings, BCG with OpenAI/Anthropic partnerships, Bain) explicitly target enterprise. $50K–$200K typical project pricing. Specialist partnerships succeed 67% of the time vs 33% internal-only builds. — Confidence: HIGH | Sources: 4 (virtasant.com, techloy.com, svitla.com, cflowapps.com)

5. **AI Readiness Trilemma is the diagnostic entry point** — Data fragmentation + talent gap + cost overrun are three simultaneous failure modes. 6-pillar readiness framework (Strategy, Infrastructure, Data quality, Governance, Culture, Talent) is the standard diagnostic structure. — Confidence: MEDIUM | Sources: 2 (lakeb2b.com, ovaledge.com/volumetree.com)

6. **LinkedIn is becoming an AI-search platform** — 75% of LinkedIn citations in LLM search responses come from Pulse articles. Image posts get 87% higher engagement. Niche specificity beats broad themes. Proof-based positioning (ROI metrics, client transformations) outperforms abstract thought leadership. — Confidence: MEDIUM | Sources: 3 (linkboost.co, mashup-communications.de, rosica.com)

7. **"Forward Deployed" consulting model emerging** — Consultant embeds in daily operations vs. project-based delivery. Niche specialists winning on outcome-based, delivery-accountable models. Higher accountability, higher retention, premium pricing potential. — Confidence: MEDIUM | Sources: 2 (techloy.com, cflowapps.com)

## Contradictions / Uncertainties

- **Market size spread**: $11B vs $14B (2026), $90B vs $116B (2035) — different research methodologies. Core direction (explosive growth) is consistent across all sources; exact figures are projections, not hard data.
- **90% failure rate**: CorporateVisions stat is dramatic and directionally correct, but not corroborated by a second independent source. Treat as indicative, not citable as verified fact.
- **$18M annual SaaS waste per enterprise**: Single-source, no methodology cited — LOW-MEDIUM confidence.
