## Salary Summary (US, April 2026)

**Senior Frontend Engineer**
- National median: $145K–$181K base; $181K–$240K total comp
- 75th percentile: $200K–$238K base
- FAANG L5: $200K–$280K base; $400K–$700K+ total
- Coinbase FE median (levels.fyi): $415K total comp
- Remote (US, non-FAANG): $150K–$200K realistic band

**Engineering Lead / Tech Lead**
- National median: $181K–$205K base; $220K–$300K total
- 75th percentile: $236K–$260K; $300K–$450K total
- FAANG EM: $250K–$350K base; $600K–$1M+ total
- Web3 infrastructure senior: $180K–$350K total + token grants

**Global (non-US)**
- W. Europe (DE/UK): $70K–$90K | Switzerland: $110K–$130K
- Singapore: $60K–$80K | India: $40K–$55K | Global avg: ~$96K

## Must-Have Skills 2026

**Non-negotiables:** TypeScript, React 18+, Next.js (App Router), CSS/Tailwind, Git/CI-CD, Vitest/Playwright, Core Web Vitals

**Senior signals:** System design for FE, micro-frontends, design system ownership, monorepo (Turborepo/Nx), backend fluency, accessibility (WCAG 2.2), performance at scale

**AI integration (rapidly becoming required):** LLM streaming UIs, OpenAI/Anthropic API integration, MCP awareness, fluent use of Cursor/Claude Code/Copilot (70% of devs use 2-4 tools simultaneously)

**Web3-specific:** ethers.js/viem/wagmi, RainbowKit/Web3Modal/Privy, smart contract ABI interactions, multi-chain UX, gas estimation patterns

## Candidate Differentiation

**Technical:** Architectural judgment (why, not just what), owns non-trivial surface area, full-stack fluency, has shipped AI-integrated product features, production-scale reliability experience

**Leadership:** Runs code review culture, translates technical tradeoffs to non-engineers, mentoring track record with outcomes, made architectural decisions that aged well

**2026-specific signal:** AI tools as multiplier + demonstrable architectural judgment the AI couldn't supply. "Here is what I built with AI as pair, here is the judgment I applied." Pure vibe coding filtered out at senior level.

**Web3 scarcity premium:** Very few senior FE engineers have real oracle/blockchain infrastructure experience. Genuine differentiator vs. every other senior FE candidate.

## AI Workflow Trends

- Claude Code #1 (46% love rate), Cursor #2 (19%), Copilot #3 (9%)
- Senior engineers get 22% velocity boost; juniors only 4% — AI amplifies existing expertise
- 40–55% more code per sprint from AI-fluent teams
- Frontend has shifted from writing to orchestrating (direct AI output, enforce design system constraints, review regressions)
- Hiring managers screen for: opinionated AI workflow answers, constraint-setting before generation (not after)

## Market Conditions

- Employer's market overall (5.8% tech unemployment, highest since dot-com era)
- 214 companies, 90K+ people cut in first ~90 days of 2026
- New SE job postings down 15% vs. same period 2025
- Median time to re-employment: 4.7 months (up from 3.2 months in 2024)
- BUT: carve-out candidate leverage for senior specialists with AI + Web3 + infrastructure depth
- "Cut and redirect" pattern: companies simultaneously laying off generalists and hiring senior AI-fluent specialists
